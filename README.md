# Privacy Policy for Frame Snap

**Last updated:** June 27, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
* **Media and Files:** Frame Snap requires storage permissions solely to read video files for frame extraction and to save the processed images back to your device. We do not transmit or upload your personal media to any external servers.

## 2. Third-Party Services & Monetization
We integrate **Google AdMob** to display advertisements within the application. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers, cookies, or tracking data to customize advertising experiences. For more information, please review the official [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 3. Data Security
Your privacy is important to us. All core video processing and image extraction happen strictly locally on your physical device.
